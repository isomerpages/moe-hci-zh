<p>We are thrilled to announce that our Hwa Chongians performed swimmingly
at the Singapore Science Olympiad across all disciplines (Biology, Chemistry
and Physics).</p>
<p>For Biology, our participants secured 1 gold and 5 merit awards. For Chemistry,
we bagged 3 gold, 10 silver, and 2 merit awards. For Physics, we achieved
6 gold, 7 silver, 3 bronze awards and 1 honourable mention.&nbsp;</p>
