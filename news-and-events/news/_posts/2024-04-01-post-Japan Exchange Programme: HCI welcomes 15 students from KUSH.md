<p>From 22 to 28 Mar 2024, Hwa Chong Institution (College Section) hosted
15 students from Kansai University Senior High (KUSH) under its Japan Exchange
Programme. This collaboration returns the warm welcome received last November
when Hwa Chong students immersed themselves in the vibrant culture and
student life of Osaka, Japan.</p>

<p>Their journey in Singapore commenced with Hwa Chongians warmly greeting
their Japanese buddies at the airport with personalised welcome boards
and dinner. In the following days, the Japanese students immersed in the
wonders Singapore has to offer through a meticulously planned tour and
lessons in school.</p>
<p>Their itinerary included visits to iconic local spots such as Gardens
by the Bay, Marina Barrage, and River Wonders. Additionally, they savoured
Singapore's diverse culinary offerings, from satay at hawker center, Satay
by the Bay, to Ice Kacang at local eatery, Mei Heong Yuen.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
