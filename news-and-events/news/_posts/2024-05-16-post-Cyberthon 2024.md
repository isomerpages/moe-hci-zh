<p>Cyberthon 2024 was an exhilarating online challenge uniting the fields
of cybersecurity and data science, jointly organised by Hwa Chong Institution
(HCI), Centre for Strategic Infocomm Technologies (CSIT), and the Digital
and Intelligence Service (DIS). This year, 628 students and teachers from
21 schools joined forces to showcase their skills.</p>
