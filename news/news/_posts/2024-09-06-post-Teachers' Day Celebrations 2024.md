<p>Hwa Chong is filled with joy and gratitude as we celebrate our teachers.
Our Teachers' Day celebrations started with our principal's inspirations
of the many hopes and dreams our educators ignite.</p>
<p>At High School, the theme, "Our Teachers, Our Stars," saw our beloved
teachers decked out in yellow, white, and sunglasses! Students hosted a
lively Teachers’ Day concert followed by heartwarming class parties to
show their appreciation.</p>

<p>Over at College, under the enchanting theme, "Once Upon a Classroom,"
our teachers made a grand entrance to a series of captivating performances
by CCAs and student groups. The event featured an awards show and a special
performance by our new teachers. Each class also took a moment to express
their appreciation to their teachers in a touching tribute session.</p>
