Singapore Police Force (SPF) Book Prize!</p>

<p>The SPF Book Prize was presented by Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP)
Darrel Long, Commanding Officer of Sembawang NPC. This award is given to
students in recognition of their academic excellence and stellar co-curricular
achievements. The award aims to motivate students in their continued pursuit
of excellence in school and affirm the SPFâ€™s commitment towards the holistic
development of students as future leaders of Singapore.</p>
<p>Every year, the SPF presents the SPF Book Prize to 20 second-year Junior
College, Year 6 International Baccalaureate and Year 2 Polytechnic Students.
As part of the SPF Book Prize journey, students will be given the rare
opportunity to participate in enriching experiential programmes that allow
them to interact with various SPF officers to get a glimpse into the exciting
world of policing. Award recipients are also awarded a cash prize of $2500.</p>
<p>The recipients for this year are:</p>
